Introduction to Kangaroos

Kangaroos are marsupials native to Australia. They are known for their strong hind legs and large tails, which they use for balance while hopping.

Where Kangaroos Live

Kangaroos are found in many parts of Australia, living in open grasslands, forests, and even deserts. They adapt to a variety of environments.

What Kangaroos Eat

Kangaroos are herbivores, primarily feeding on grasses, leaves, and shrubs. Their diet depends on the availability of vegetation in their habitat.

Kangaroo Behavior

Kangaroos are known for their hopping motion, which is a result of their powerful hind legs. They also live in groups called mobs and are social animals.
